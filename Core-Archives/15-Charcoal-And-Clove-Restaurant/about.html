About Us

Charcoal & Clove started as a single charcoal grill on a street corner in 2016. Today it's a full table experience built around the same idea: real fire, real spice, no shortcuts. Every dish leaves the kitchen the way it would at our founder's own table.

We work directly with local growers and spice traders for everything that hits the grill, and our menu changes with what's actually in season rather than what's easiest to stock. Slow-cooked, hand-rolled, and always finished over charcoal.
